What We Analyze

Our AI extracts 10 handwriting features including:

  • Stroke smoothness: Continuity and flow
  • Line variation: Thickness consistency
  • Pattern regularity: Spacing uniformity
  • Tremor detection: Micro-oscillations
  • Size consistency: Micrographia indicators
Clinical Indicators

Parkinson's handwriting changes:

  • Micrographia: Writing becomes progressively smaller
  • Tremor: Shaky, irregular lines
  • Slow movement: Reduced writing speed
  • Poor spacing: Letters crowd together
  • Pressure variation: Inconsistent pen pressure

What We Analyze

Our AI extracts 10 gait features including:

  • Step frequency: Steps per second
  • Stride length: Distance per step
  • Movement smoothness: Flow consistency
  • Balance metrics: Body sway patterns
  • Arm swing amplitude: Natural movement range
Parkinson's Gait Signs

Key indicators in walking patterns:

  • Shuffling: Small, dragging steps
  • Reduced arm swing: Arms stay closer to body
  • Freezing episodes: Sudden inability to move
  • Festinating gait: Accelerating, hunched walk
  • Poor balance: Increased fall risk
